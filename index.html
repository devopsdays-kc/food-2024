Food Menu

Each day we'll have a different menu, from 3 of Kansas City's finest food trucks.

Wednesday, May 15, 2024

The Hungry Hatch
  • The Indian: Chickpeas, sliced carrots, and cut red peppers in a coconut curry sauce on rice (Vegan)
  • The Thai: Chicken, sliced purple cabbage, grated carrots, edamame, sliced red peppers topped with peanuts and spicy sweet chili sauce (can be Vegan w/o chicken)
  • The Southwest: Chicken, black beans, corn, grilled trio bell peppers and onions topped with cheddar jack cheese
All of the above can be in a Bowl / Wrap / or Salad
Drink: Soda or Bottled Water

Taste of Brazil
  • Shredded Chicken bowl with rice, beans, chimichurri
  • Shredded Chicken sandwich with fries
  • Brazilian Pork Sausage bowl with rice, beans, chimichurri
  • Brazilian Pork Sausage sandwich with fries
  • BBQ Chicken sandwich with fries
  • Chicken Nachos
Drink: Soda or Bottled Water

Mai's Noodles
  • Teriyaki Chicken with Fried Rice and/or Noodles
  • Teriyaki with Steamed Rice
  • Fried Rice (Vegan)
  • Stir Fried Yakisoba Noodles (Vegan)
Drink: Soda or Bottled Water

Thursday, May 16, 2024

Taco Republic

Marigolds
